"This is a volley rain": akim of Karaganda explained the reasons for the flood in the city

According to the mayor, all the shower receivers in the city are in working condition.
And the flooding of the streets occurred due to a volley of rain and foliage, which clogged into the lattice covers of some shower channels.

"I want to say that this rain is not ordinary, it is a volley rain, with a lot of precipitation.
The clouds have been hanging over our city for an hour, and we can't say, what is the current norm for an hour"

- said the akim of Karaganda, addressing the residents of the city.
